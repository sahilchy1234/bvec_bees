# Beezy Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 22 November 2025_

Beezy ("we", "our", or "us") is a campus community app for BVEC students. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use:

- The Beezy mobile apps (Android and other platforms)
- The Beezy website and link pages, including `https://link.getbeezy.app` and `https://edunova-fa954.web.app`

By installing or using Beezy, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

> **Note:** This document is a general template and may need to be reviewed by a legal professional to ensure compliance with your local laws and Google Play policies.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Beezy.

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account information**  
  When you sign up or sign in, we may collect your:
  - Name or display name
  - Email address or phone number
  - Profile picture (if you choose to upload one)

- **Profile and settings**  
  You may optionally provide additional profile details and preferences.

- **Content you create**  
  When you use Beezy, you may create or interact with:
  - Posts, comments, reactions, and other content
  - Rumors and anonymous posts
  - Messages and other in‑app interactions

  Some of this content may be anonymous to other users (for example, rumors), but may still be associated with an internal identifier for safety, moderation, or abuse prevention.

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the app or website, we automatically collect:

- **Device and log information**  
  - Device model, operating system version
  - App version
  - IP address (short‑term for security and diagnostics)
  - Crash logs and performance data

- **Usage information**  
  - Screens and features you use
  - Links you open (for example, post or rumor deep links)
  - Basic interaction events (e.g. open app, open notification)

### 1.3 Information from Cookies and Similar Technologies

On the web, we may use cookies or similar technologies to:

- Maintain your session
- Remember certain preferences
- Improve performance and security

You can manage cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

### 1.4 Information from Notifications and Deep Links

- When you tap **notifications** or **shared links** (e.g. `https://link.getbeezy.app/post/<id>` or `https://link.getbeezy.app/rumor/<id>`), we may log:
  - That a notification or link was opened
  - Which post or rumor was opened

This helps us ensure deep links work correctly and improve the app experience.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and operate the service**  
   - Create and manage user accounts
   - Deliver posts, rumors, comments, and other content
   - Support features like likes, votes, and comments

2. **Enable campus community features**  
   - Show you relevant feed content
   - Allow you to post, comment, and interact with others
   - Support anonymous rumor posting while still enabling moderation and abuse prevention

3. **Improve and protect Beezy**  
   - Monitor performance and fix bugs
   - Detect and prevent spam, abuse, or violations of our community guidelines
   - Analyze usage trends to improve features and safety

4. **Send notifications and updates**  
   - Notify you about replies, reactions, new content, or important app updates
   - You can control some notification settings in the app and at the system level.

5. **Comply with legal obligations**  
   - Respond to valid legal requests
   - Enforce our Terms of Use and community guidelines

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell your personal data.

We may share information in these limited cases:

1. **Service Providers**  
   We use third‑party services (such as Google/Firebase) for:
   - Data storage and databases
   - Authentication
   - Cloud messaging and notifications
   - Analytics and crash reporting

   These providers process data on our behalf and are required to protect it.

2. **Other Users**  
   - Content you post (posts, comments, rumors) will be visible to other users according to the app’s features and settings (for example, rumors may be shown anonymously).

3. **Legal and Safety**  
   We may disclose information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to:
   - Comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or government request
   - Protect the safety, rights, or property of users, Beezy, or others
   - Investigate and address fraud, security, or technical issues

4. **Business Transfers**  
   If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will make reasonable efforts to notify you of any such change.

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

- Your data is stored using cloud infrastructure (for example, Firebase/Google Cloud) which may be located in different regions.
- We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the service and for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
- We may retain certain logs or backups for a limited time for security, fraud prevention, or legal reasons, even after account deletion or content removal.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights such as:

- **Access** – Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction** – Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion** – Request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal exceptions).
- **Restriction or objection** – Request that we limit or stop certain processing.
- **Withdraw consent** – Where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in the **Contact Us** section below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

You can also:

- Adjust app‑level preferences in Beezy where available.
- Control system‑level permissions (for example, notifications) in your device settings.
